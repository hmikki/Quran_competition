Our Upbringing

Praise be to God, and prayers and peace be upon the Messenger of God, and after...
Among the things God honored the nation of Muhammad, may God bless him and grant him peace, was that He revealed to them an Arabic Qur’an, with which God guides those who follow His pleasure. Ways of peace, and ease for the nation’s affairs by following the guidance of its Prophet Muhammad, may God bless him and grant him peace, so that no one will deviate from it except those who are perishing.
Indeed, one of the features of the right path and the straight path is serving the Book of God Almighty, with which God honored this blessed country and made it its constitution, so the competition was established for memorizing the Holy Qur’an.
The competition for memorizing the Holy Quran was established in 3241 AH, aiming to spread the teachings of the Holy Quran. All of this would not have been possible without the grace of God Almighty, then the joined hands of administrators and teachers, and the combined efforts of donors and benefactors.

Our Vision

Excellence in serving the Book of God Almighty in its recitation, memorization, contemplation, and work.

Our Message

We serve the Book of God Almighty with high efficiency to be the best in quality Quranic programs directed to segments of society with qualified cadres who work in a team spirit in a competitive work environment.

Our Goals

The competition aims to memorize the Holy Qur’an to fulfill what was promised to this nation in the words of the Prophet, may God bless him and grant him peace: (The best of you are those who learn and teach the Qur’an) Sahih Al-Bukhari, through the following:

Teaching the Holy Qur’an in recitation, memorization, contemplation and intonation

Creating proficient memorizers who adhere to the morals of the Holy Qur’an

Qualifying male and female teachers to teach the Holy Quran

Contributing to fortifying learners intellectually
